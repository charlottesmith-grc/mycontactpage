I’m Charlotte Smith, a designer and artist from Boyce, Virginia. I currently live in San Luis Obispo and am pursuing a degree in Graphic Communications. Prior to switching tracks to focus on a more visual format of design, I had two years of education through Cal Poly’s Mechanical Engineering program. I love creative problem solving and thrive off of creating art in a variety of mediums. In particular I enjoy drawing, watercolor, fashion design, sewing and pottery. I really enjoy learning new skills that allow me to create in different formats- my most recent venture being this website!
